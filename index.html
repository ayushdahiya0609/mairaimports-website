About Us

Australia's Dedicated Indian Goods Importer & Wholesaler

Maira Imports Pty Ltd is a registered Australian company specialising in the import, wholesale and distribution of authentic Indian groceries and cultural products. We bridge the gap between Indian manufacturers and Australian businesses, delivering quality at competitive wholesale prices.

Whether you're a retailer stocking Indian products, a restaurant sourcing ingredients, or a business looking for cultural and festival merchandise — Maira Imports is your end-to-end supply partner.
